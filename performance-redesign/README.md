# Ecommerce Redesign Strategy

This folder is a standalone storefront concept based on the KPI dashboard signals in the parent project.

## Dashboard Signals Used

- Net revenue is up, but conversion rate is down by 0.3 points.
- Direct traffic performs better than paid traffic, while paid media needs tighter efficiency.
- Newark fulfillment is critical, and APAC has a service-level drag.
- Hydra Bottle and Trail Pack are stockout or reorder risks.
- Core Tee has overstock exposure and is better used in bundles.

## Strategies Applied

- Put best sellers, delivery confidence, and add-to-cart actions in the first shopping path.
- Add stock-aware product messaging for scarce products before checkout.
- Use bundles to pair high-demand items with overstocked inventory and lift AOV.
- Adapt fulfillment messaging by region so shoppers see realistic delivery expectations earlier.
- Favor direct and retention-friendly merchandising instead of relying on more paid traffic.
